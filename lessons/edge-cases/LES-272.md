**Pattern sai:**
```php
echo '<span class="text-sm text-gray-500">(' . esc_html( $count ) . ')</span>';
```

**Phải làm:**
```php
echo '<span class="text-sm text-gray-500">(' . esc_html( wz_abbreviate_number( $count, 'reviews' ) ) . ')</span>';
```

**Quy tắc:** Reviews > 9,999 → hiển thị "9.9K+" thay vì "12,345".
