## Bad
```tsx
'Hỗ trợ kỹ thuật trọn đời'
'Bảo hành trọn đời'
'Lifetime technical support'
```
Indefinite service commitment without scope/conditions violates Google "Unrealistic Promises" and Meta "Misleading Claims" policies.

## Good
```tsx
'Hỗ trợ kỹ thuật 12 tháng miễn phí'
'Bảo hành 12 tháng — gia hạn 200.000đ/tháng'
```
Specify exact duration + what happens after. "Sở hữu trọn đời" for source code/license is OK (tangible asset), but "hỗ trợ trọn đời" for services is not (ongoing labor commitment).

## Why
No business can guarantee indefinite service delivery. Google considers this "unrealistic" because the company could close, change pricing, or discontinue support. The claim creates a binding obligation that cannot be fulfilled indefinitely. Fix: convert to specific timeframe + optional renewal.
