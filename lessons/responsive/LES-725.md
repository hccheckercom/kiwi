## Bad
```php
<div class="grid grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-4 gap-4">
  <?php foreach ($products as $product): ?>
    <?php get_template_part('template-parts/product/card', null, $product); ?>
  <?php endforeach; ?>
</div>
```
Tablet (768px) vẫn hiện 2 cột — quá ít cho iPad.

## Good
```php
<div class="grid grid-cols-2 md:grid-cols-3 lg:grid-cols-4 gap-4">
  <?php foreach ($products as $product): ?>
    <?php get_template_part('template-parts/product/card', null, $product); ?>
  <?php endforeach; ?>
</div>
```

## Why
Product grid trong WP theme phải có md: breakpoint. Tablet users chiếm 15-20% traffic ecommerce — layout phải tối ưu cho họ.
