## Bad
```tsx
'Giúp sản phẩm được duyệt nhanh, tránh bị suspend'
'Đảm bảo tài khoản Google Ads không bị ban'
'Cam kết website pass GMC review'
```
Implying you can control Google/Meta's moderation decisions violates "Misrepresentation of Capabilities" policy.

## Good
```tsx
'Tối ưu feed theo chuẩn GMC để giảm thiểu rủi ro từ chối'
'Tuân thủ chính sách Google Ads để hạn chế vi phạm'
'Audit website theo checklist GMC compliance'
```

## Why
No third party can guarantee approval by Google or Meta — these platforms make independent moderation decisions. Claiming otherwise is "misrepresentation of capabilities" and can trigger account suspension for the advertiser AND the service provider. Use language that describes what YOU do (optimize, audit, comply) rather than what the PLATFORM will do (approve, not suspend).
