## Bad
```tsx
<div className="border border-gray-200 rounded-lg">
  <ul className="divide-y divide-gray-200">
    <li>Item 1</li>
    <li>Item 2</li>
  </ul>
</div>
```
Light gray borders disappear against dark backgrounds — elements merge visually.

## Good
```tsx
<div className="border border-gray-200 dark:border-gray-700 rounded-lg">
  <ul className="divide-y divide-gray-200 dark:divide-gray-700">
    <li>Item 1</li>
    <li>Item 2</li>
  </ul>
</div>
```

## Why
`border-gray-200` (#e5e7eb) on `dark:bg-gray-800` (#1f2937) has contrast ratio < 1.5:1 — effectively invisible. Borders and dividers need darker variants to maintain visual separation in dark mode.
