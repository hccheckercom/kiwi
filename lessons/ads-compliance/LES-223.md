## Bad
```tsx
// Footer only shows:
<p>Wezone.vn</p>
<p>313 Đường E, Buôn Ma Thuột</p>
<p>0393 331 334</p>
// No legal entity name, no MST, no registration number
```
Google Advertiser Verification requires verifiable business identity. Missing = account hold during verification process.

## Good
```tsx
<div className="text-sm text-gray-400">
  <p>Công ty TNHH Wezone Việt Nam</p>
  <p>MST: 6001234567</p>
  <p>ĐKKD số: 41A8012345 — Sở KH&ĐT Đắk Lắk</p>
  <p>Địa chỉ: 313 Đường E, Buôn Ma Thuột, Đắk Lắk</p>
</div>
```

## Why
Google Ads Advertiser Verification (mandatory since 2023) requires: (1) legal business name matching registration, (2) business registration number or tax ID, (3) physical address. Without these, Google will hold the account during verification (up to 30 days, no ads running). Meta Business Verification similarly requires official documents. Adding MST proactively prevents verification delays.
