## Bad
```tsx
<div className="shadow-sm rounded-lg">Card A</div>
<div className="shadow-lg rounded-lg">Card B</div>
<div className="shadow-xl rounded-lg">Card C</div>
```
3 shadow levels khác nhau cho cards cùng level — visual depth không nhất quán.

## Good
```tsx
<div className="shadow-sm rounded-lg">Card A</div>
<div className="shadow-sm rounded-lg">Card B</div>
<div className="shadow-sm rounded-lg">Card C</div>
```

## Why
Shadow scale: shadow-sm cho cards, shadow-md cho modals/dropdowns, shadow-lg cho popovers. Mixing random shadows phá vỡ depth hierarchy.
