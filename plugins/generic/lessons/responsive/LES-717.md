## Bad
```tsx
<div className="flex flex-row gap-4">
  <FilterPanel />
  <ProductList />
  <CartSummary />
</div>
```
3 items in a row without wrap — overflows on mobile.

## Good
```tsx
<div className="flex flex-col gap-4 md:flex-row">
  <FilterPanel />
  <ProductList />
  <CartSummary />
</div>
```
Stack vertically on mobile, row on desktop.

## Why
`flex-row` with multiple children and no `flex-wrap` or responsive `flex-col` will overflow on narrow screens. Navigation and breadcrumbs are exceptions (they have their own overflow handling).
