**Mô tả:**
- Hero/LCP image PHẢI có `fetchpriority="high"` + `loading="eager"`
- Product images PHẢI có `srcset` + `sizes` cho responsive loading
- Above-fold images KHÔNG được `loading="lazy"`

**Detect pattern (absence):**
- Hero/homepage templates thiếu `fetchpriority` hoặc `srcset` → LCP chậm, ảnh không responsive

**Code mẫu:**
```html
<!-- Hero LCP image -->
<img src="hero-800.webp"
  srcset="hero-400.webp 400w, hero-800.webp 800w, hero-1200.webp 1200w"
  sizes="100vw"
  fetchpriority="high"
  loading="eager"
  width="1440" height="600"
  alt="...">
```

**Budget từ blueprint:**
- Hero/Banner: max 1440px width, 80% quality, max 120KB
- Product Card Thumb: max 400px, 80% quality, max 40KB
- Total images above fold: < 500KB (homepage)

**Lợi ích:** LCP < 2.0s, responsive images giảm bandwidth 50-70% trên mobile
