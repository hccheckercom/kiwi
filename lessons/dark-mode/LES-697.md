## Bad
```tsx
<div className="bg-white p-6 rounded-lg shadow">
  <h2 className="text-gray-900">Dashboard</h2>
</div>
```
White background in dark mode = blinding flash, broken visual hierarchy.

## Good
```tsx
<div className="bg-white dark:bg-gray-800 p-6 rounded-lg shadow dark:shadow-gray-900/20">
  <h2 className="text-gray-900 dark:text-gray-100">Dashboard</h2>
</div>
```
Every light background has a dark counterpart.

## Why
Users who enable dark mode expect consistent dark surfaces. A single `bg-white` without `dark:` creates a jarring white rectangle that breaks the dark theme and can cause eye strain in low-light environments.
